Bio

Hi, I’m Sahana Balaji, a Computer Science student at Michigan State University, where I spend my time figuring out how to make code behave (and sometimes questioning why it won’t). I grew up in Dubai, a city where you can spot futuristic skyscrapers and traditional markets within a few blocks—probably why I love both tech and culture.

I’m all about tackling cool projects, from predicting stock market trends to building systems that solve real-world problems. I enjoy the process of turning a messy problem into a neat solution (and the satisfaction when the code finally works).

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me planning my next trip, binge-watching sci-fi, or convincing myself that coffee counts as a hobby. I’m always looking for the next thing to learn or build, and I’m excited to see where this journey takes me!

My hobbies:

  • Listening to music
  • Cooking
  • Coding
  • Exploring new places
  • Do things to others and myself happy
